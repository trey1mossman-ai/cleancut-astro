Our Deck Restoration Process

1

Deep Cleansing

Pressure washing, hand scrubbing, and rinsing to remove dirt, mildew, and contaminants

2

Surface Refinement

Orbital sanding eliminates loose surfaces and opens wood grain for better stain penetration

3

Structural Integrity

Replacement of rotted and irreparable boards

4

Stability Reinforcement

Repair and re-securing of loose boards

5

Sealing Perfection

New caulking applied to gaps and voids for weather protection

6

Color Transformation

Two coats of high-quality exterior solid color stain

7

Final Walkthrough

Project completion with your approval and warranty commitment
